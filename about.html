About Us

We believe in simplifying your finances through value based accounting practices.

Let us deal with the complications of your accounting, it’s what we’re good at.

Our Way

Value Based Pricing

Our approach is to bundle our services for a fixed cost per month, tailored to the needs of your business, this way you pay for the value of our services and avoid any surprises based on a ticking clock

We don’t lock you into a long-term contract, you can cancel anytime by giving us 30 days’ notice.

Automation

We leverage off the best tools of the trade to automate your business processes as much as possible.

The result is timely and reliable financial information that doesn’t take an excessive amount of time to prepare, rather spend your time working on your business than working in your business.

Professional

We are a team of skilled financial professionals registered with SAICA, committed to delivering a gold standard of expertise and service to our clients.

Business Partner

While we provide accounting, tax and compliance services, all the things we enjoy doing, that’s just the start, what sets us apart is how we partner with your business.

Think of Levistin as an extension of your business, not an accountant that you hear from once or twice a year but someone who treats your business like we treat ours and is always available to help.

Our Values
